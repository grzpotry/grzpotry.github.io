Grzegorz Potrykus

About

Over 8 years of experience in game software development as programmer.
8 years in Unity and 2 years in Unreal Engine on which I am now mostly focused on.

With a wealth of experience in transforming design concepts into gameplay, I thrive when tackling new challenges and realizing ambitious visions.

Worked as programmer in small team from very beginning on successful indie rpg PC title - Tower of Time.
After successful launch I continued as senior programmer on much more ambitious production - Dark Envoy.
Recently I worked on unannounced action RPG title on Unreal Engine 5, heavily utilizing GAS system and Lyra project.

Consider myself as generalist programmer - extensive experience in a rapidly growing studio required wearing many hats and engaging in various phases of the production process, from conceptualization, through rough development - to the final releases.

Extensive practical experience in such areas as procedural generation, characters AI, quest mechanics, performance optimization (CPU, multithreading, memory usage), editor tools, continuous integration, 3rd party tools integration & maintenance. Supported multiplayer & networking features.

Leaded quest scripting team for 2 years - in parallel to coding duties.
